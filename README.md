# BlankCheckModel
A hierchical diff-eq model and 15,000 Monte Carlo Simulations that demonstrate the conditions that lead to the end of Blank-Check US Aid to Israel and the crash of the Tel Aviv real estate market. Model predicts from 2032-2038 that the aid will dry up.
